Our Mission

The Aggie Sailing Club is dedicated to promoting the sport of sailing and fostering a welcoming community of sailors. We believe that sailing is more than just a sportâ€”it's a way of life that teaches valuable skills, builds character, and creates lasting friendships.

Our mission is to make sailing accessible to everyone, regardless of experience level. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced racer, we provide the resources, training, and support you need to enjoy the thrill of sailing.

Our History

1999

Club Founded

The Aggie Sailing Club was established by a group of passionate students who wanted to share their love of sailing with the university community. The initial facility was at Lake Somerville (28 miles from College Station).

2005

First Fleet Purchase

We acquired our first fleet of boats, making sailing accessible to more students!

2010

Clubhouse Construction

We moved to Lake Bryan, our current home. A dedicated clubhouse was built, providing a central location for meetings, training, and social events.

2015

Racing Program Launch

We competed in our first nationals in 2015.

2021

Fleet Expansion

Replaced entire fleet of 420s, purchasing a slightly used fleet from a northeast university.

2024

25th Anniversary

Celebrating 25 years of sailing excellence and community building at Texas A&M University.
