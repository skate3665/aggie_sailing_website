Getting Here

By Car: Take Highway 6 to Lake Drive exit. Turn right onto Lake Drive and continue for 2 miles. The club is on the left.

By Bus: Take Aggie Spirit Bus Route 15 to Lake Drive stop. Walk 5 minutes to the club.

Parking: Free parking available on-site for members and visitors.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

You can become a member by filling out our online application form on the Membership page, or by visiting us in person during club hours. We offer different membership tiers to suit your needs.

Do I need sailing experience to join?

No sailing experience is required! We welcome sailors of all skill levels and provide comprehensive training programs for beginners.

What equipment do I need to bring?

We provide all necessary sailing equipment including life jackets, boats, and safety gear. You just need to bring appropriate clothing for the weather.

Can I rent boats if I'm not a member?

Boat rentals are available to members only. However, we offer introductory sailing sessions for non-members who want to try sailing before joining.

What are the club hours?

We're open weekdays from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM and weekends from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Hours may vary during holidays and special events.

Do you offer sailing lessons?

Yes! We offer comprehensive sailing lessons for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced racing techniques. Check our Events page for upcoming training sessions.
