Set Sail with Us

Whether you're a seasoned sailor or just starting your journey on the water, the Aggie Sailing Club offers something for everyone. From competitive racing to leisurely cruises, we provide opportunities to develop your skills, make lasting friendships, and experience the pure joy of sailing.

Our club welcomes members of all experience levels, from complete beginners to experienced racers. We offer training programs, social events, and access to our fleet of boats.
